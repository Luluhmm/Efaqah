Medical Disclaimer

Please read this information carefully before using Efaqah.

Clinical Decision Support

Efaqah is a clinical decision support tool designed to assist medical professionals. It is not a replacement for clinical judgment, comprehensive patient evaluation, or specialist consultation. Final diagnosis and treatment decisions remain the responsibility of the treating clinician.

Model Performance

On internal validation cohorts, Efaqah’s models achieved approximate accuracies of 97% (CT imaging classifier) and 92% (symptom-based risk model). Performance may vary across populations, scanners, imaging protocols, and clinical settings.

Intended Use & Limitations

  • Supports rapid risk stratification using non-contrast CT and structured clinical features.
  • Not intended to be used as the sole basis for a diagnosis, therapy, or discharge decision.
  • Outputs (scores, labels, bands) should be interpreted within full clinical context.
  • Edge cases, atypical presentations, and data quality issues can affect results.

Emergency & Safety

In suspected stroke emergencies, follow institutional protocols and time-critical pathways (e.g., stroke code activation). Do not delay urgent care while awaiting Efaqah outputs.

Regulatory Status

Efaqah is provided for clinical decision support and evaluation purposes. Regulatory clearance or certification may vary by jurisdiction and deployment model. Use is subject to site governance and approval.

Data Handling

Data should be handled according to your institution’s privacy, security, and retention policies. Ensure appropriate authorization for any uploads and access.
