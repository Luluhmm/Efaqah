MEET EFAQAH

Efaqah is an innovative platform aimed at early detection of strokes, developed by students from Tuwaiq Academy. The platform helps doctors and users make quick and effective health decisions. We believe in the importance of awareness and early screening to reduce complications and protect lives.

OUR VISION

At Efaqah, our vision is to revolutionize healthcare by leveraging technology for early detection of strokes. We aim to empower both doctors and patients with intelligent tools that enable quick decision-making, raise awareness, and ultimately save lives through timely intervention.
