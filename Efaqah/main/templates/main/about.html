MEET EFAQAH

Efaqah is a Saudi-built clinical AI platform for early stroke detection and decision support. Developed by a young, passionate Tuwaiq Academy team, it supports neurologists, emergency physicians, and radiologists with rapid risk stratification from non-contrast CT and structured clinical features helping streamline triage and time-critical decisions. We champion early screening and awareness to reduce complications and protect lives.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to advance stroke care across the region with safe, clinician-centered AI. We aim to empower doctors and patients with reliable tools for rapid assessment, transparent risk scoring, and confident decisions so more lives are protected through earlier intervention.
